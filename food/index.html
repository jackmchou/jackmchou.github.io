Getting Started

  1. Cut vegetable and or tofu/meat to preferred size/shape
  2. Wash barley and or other grains if preferred
  3. Fill pot with water
  4. Barley in pot first (If meat is used, put in the meat at this step)
  5. Put in vegetables
  6. Put tofu and seasoning
  7. Grains in last
  8. Additional water/ingredients if preferred or additional space available
  9. Cap pressure cooker & ensure seal is secure
  10. Set on high-pressure manual at 1 - 5 minutes(If meat is used, cook time will need to double to 10-20 mins)
