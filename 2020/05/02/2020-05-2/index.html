  • When a user add an item (from the item details) to their cart, they should be presented with the option to “Continue Shopping” (navigates to product list) and either “View Cart” (navigates to cart) or “Checkout” (navigates to checkout).
    • These options should be easy to notice, near the center of the page.
  • Cart items should support any positive integer quantity.
    • Duplicate items in cart should be merged, unless the items are somehow “customized” by the user.
    • Quantities should be modifiable by incrementing/decrementing or by directly choosing a value. Quantities below (1) should be prevented.
    • To remove item from their cart, the user must click a “Remove from Cart” button.
  • Item removal from cart should require confirmation of deleted from user.
    • Confirmation prompt should identify which item the user is deleting.
    • Example: "Are you sure you want to remove ${qty} of ${itemName} from your cart?"
  • The checkout form should meet the Form Component Spec.
  • User must be presented with an Order Confirmation or Thank You after successfully completing checkout.
